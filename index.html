Trust & FAQs

Your comfort and peace of mind are always our top priority

Are you pet friendly?

Yes, absolutely! We love pets. We only use safe, non-toxic products so your cats, dogs, or other animals are completely safe and comfortable during and after our cleaning.

Do I need to be home?

No, you don't have to be. Most of our clients prefer to give us code entry or leave a key. We are fully trusted, professional, and treats your home with the utmost care and security.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, TT's Cleaning is a fully registered, licensed, and insured business in Alberta. You can have absolute peace of mind having us in your home.

What if I need to cancel or change my day?

Life happens! We offer free rescheduling and cancellations up to 24 hours before your booking. Just shoot us a quick text or email and we will re-arrange things for you.
